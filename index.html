They occasionally and unintentionally gently brushed each other's needles, but that aside, stayed to themselves, swaying side to side leisurely. I stared at them as if I had never seen a pine tree in my life (as I do with many things), and can recall every detail of their appearance.

The branches curved upward and were layered on top of each other, spiraling up perfectly to the pinnacle, long twigs sheltering them.
